The Floritech Innovation

Floritech Organo Industries, established in 2015 at Nagpur, is a pioneer in the agricultural sector. We have dedicated ourselves to the development of unique, highly effective organic farming solutions that bridge the gap between traditional practices and modern scientific requirements.

Our flagship innovation involves the collection of waste human hair—a rich source of keratin and amino acids. Through our proprietary engineered process, we convert this organic waste into a highly beneficial liquid fertilizer. This process not only solves a significant waste management issue but also provides farmers with a potent, bio-available nutritional input that dramatically improves soil health and crop yield.
